Hi I'm Doris Anumba and this is my Tech Journey....

Welcome to my tech journey! In this simple website I am gonna tell you a lil bit about my tech journey.

Honestly,I have always been the kind of person who loves trying new things, learning new skills, and pushing myself out of the usual box. So when I decided to get into tech, I sat down and asked myself

What part of tech actually fits me?

At first, I thought maybe I’d go into design. I mean, I can use tools and make designs? Not really my thing even though I know my way around Canva.But the idea of building apps, games, or websites? That vibe pulled me in heavy. So I started exploring. At one point, I even took a course on the Internet of Things (IoT),Typical me just trying to figure out where I truly belonged. It was interesting, but still didn’t feel like “me.” That’s how I slowly started leaning toward coding.

At first, I wasn’t even sure which direction to take, frontend or backend and honestly? I’m still figuring that part out. But I’m in it for the long haul. Last year, I tried backend through a Zuri Training program.I was ready... then life said, “Hold up.” NYSC camp came around, and I lost three whole weeks. After camp, everything got hectic settling into a PPA, figuring out life again and just like that, the momentum was gone. I didn’t finish. I barely scratched the surface. But I didn’t let that stop me.

Fast forward to this year — post-service, post-chaos — I picked up where I left off. I started teaching myself through YouTube channels like TraversyMedia and a site called FreeCodeCamp. It was clicking. It was working.

Now, I’m here with UpSkill by Genesys , and it feels like a real step forward. It hasn’t been easy.Coming from a medical background, everything feels brand new. The tasks stretch me. Sometimes I feel stuck. But I actually love the challenge.

And even though I’m still learning, I’m proud of how far I’ve come.God willing, I’ll complete this program, move up into Learnable, and become a developer that can build beautiful, useful things whether that’s websites, apps, or something even bigger.

Thanks for reading my story and this is just the beginning.

Well That’s a wrap on my tech journey so far, and I’d like to hear about yours!
